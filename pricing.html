Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and features

Are there any setup or monthly fees?

No, there are no setup fees, monthly fees, or hidden costs. You only pay for successful transactions. We believe in transparent pricing that grows with your business.

What payment methods do you support?

We support all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay, Samsung Pay), bank transfers, and local payment methods specific to each region.

How does your fraud protection work?

Our AI-powered fraud detection system analyzes every transaction in real-time, using machine learning to identify suspicious patterns. This helps reduce chargebacks while maintaining a high approval rate for legitimate transactions.

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any billing differences. You can also use different plans for different businesses.

What kind of support do you provide?

All plans include email support. Professional plans get 24/7 priority support with faster response times. Enterprise customers receive dedicated account management and phone support. We also have extensive documentation and community forums.

Is my data secure with DGS-Pay?

Absolutely. We're PCI DSS Level 1 certified and use bank-level encryption. We never store sensitive card data on our servers and comply with all major security standards including SOC 2 Type II, GDPR, and CCPA.
